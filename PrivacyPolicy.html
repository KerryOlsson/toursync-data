Privacy Policy for TourSync

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

TourSync LLC (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the TourSync mobile application (the “App”).

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

TourSync does not collect, store, or share personal information from users.

The App may access the following features solely to support its functionality:

  • Photo Library (if the user chooses to attach notes)
  • Local device storage for tour data and user notes

No personal data is transmitted to external servers by TourSync.

2. Data Storage

All data used by the App (including notes, configurations, and tour content) is stored locally on the user’s device or retrieved from publicly available content sources configured by the organization using the app.

We do not maintain user accounts or store user data on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may retrieve content from external sources (such as GitHub-hosted files) configured by the organization using the app. These services are controlled by the organization, not TourSync LLC.

4. Data Sharing

TourSync does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

5. Security

Because data is stored locally on the device, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for use by museum staff and volunteers. It is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
